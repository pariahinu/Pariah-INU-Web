Ecosystem

Pariah-INU is an experiment in a decentralized coin meeting spontaneous community building. Pariah-INU is one of first, but many, tokens that allows users to hold billions of them. Launched Nov 1st of 2021, this BEP-20 ONLY token can remain well under a penny and still keep pace with the explosive growth of coins like Dogecoin or SHIB in a small amount of time!

50% of total supply was burned to Vitalik Buterin,the co-founder of Ethereum. 1 trillion token will be burning in every 15 days till the total supply will not come under 1 trillion, remaining tokens are locked on Pancakeswap & Uniswap.

Community

Pariah INU was created by the community and is completely decentralized. The project is decentralized, participative, and team-based, with anonymous contributors participating in the development and growth of Pariah INU.

MEME-Based

Pariah INU is a meme-based cryptocurrency, born on the internet, but with strong potential. More importantly, it is a fully decentralized, community-based cryptocurrency. Although the token is considered to be a meme-token, we fully believe in this community experiment..
